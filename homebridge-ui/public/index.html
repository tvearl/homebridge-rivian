Enrolled key:

    Enable vehicle control

    Commands (lock, climate, windows, etc.) need a one-time Bluetooth pairing with your vehicle. Do it once from a laptop/desktop running Chrome or Edge (or Android Chrome), brought near the vehicle. iPhone/iPad browsers can't do Bluetooth, so they can't pair - but once paired, control works everywhere, including your iPhone. Reading status works without pairing.

    Disconnecting removes the phone key from your Rivian account (freeing the slot) and deletes the saved session on this machine.

    Sign in to Rivian

    Your password is sent only to Rivian and is never stored.

    Enter verification code

    Rivian sent a one-time code to your email or phone.

    Choose vehicles

    A phone key will be enrolled for each selected vehicle (uses 1 of your 2 key slots per vehicle).
